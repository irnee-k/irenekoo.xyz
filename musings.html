questions I'm mulling over

  • How do we build learning systems that adapt to individual curiosity rather than standardized outcomes?
  • What does meaningful work look like when AI can do most knowledge tasks?
  • Can economic mobility exist without geographical mobility in the age of remote work?
  • Why do we still organize education around time (semesters, years) instead of mastery?
  • What happens to middle managers when coordination becomes trivial?
  • How do we measure learning that happens outside traditional institutions?
  • Is the credential or the learning more valuable? And to whom?
